  • Explore Palermo

    Explore Palermo

    Palermo, one of Buenos Aires’ most vibrant and expansive neighborhoods, is a dynamic area that truly embodies the spirit of the city. Divided into sub-districts like Palermo Soho and Palermo Hollywood, each with its own unique character, Palermo offers something for everyone. The neighborhood is well-known for its lush parks, such as the Bosques de Palermo, a sprawling green space where both locals and tourists come to relax, picnic, or enjoy outdoor activities like biking and rowing.

  • Explore Belgrano

    Explore Belgrano

    Belgrano is a charming residential neighborhood in Buenos Aires that offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city. Known for its wide, tree-lined avenues, Belgrano exudes a serene atmosphere that is ideal for families and those seeking a quieter lifestyle. The neighborhood's streets are beautifully designed, featuring a mix of elegant homes, modern apartments, and stately mansions that reflect its rich architectural heritage.

  • Explore Caballito

    Explore Caballito

    Caballito, situated in the heart of Buenos Aires, is a neighborhood that beautifully blends historical charm with vibrant urban life. Known for its picturesque parks and tree-lined streets, Caballito offers a unique ambiance that attracts both locals and visitors alike. The neighborhood is characterized by its lovely parks, including Parque Centenario, which serves as a central green space for the community.

  • Explore Núñez

    Explore Núñez

    Núñez is a serene and inviting neighborhood in Buenos Aires, often considered a hidden gem among the city’s vibrant districts. Known for its calm residential streets, Núñez provides a peaceful oasis away from the hustle and bustle of urban life. This area is characterized by its tree-lined avenues, charming homes, and a relaxed atmosphere that appeals to families and professionals alike.
